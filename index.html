⚙️ Settings

Billing cycle dates per card

💳 Billing Cycle Dates
The billing date is when your card statement is generated each month.
Spend from prev month's billing date → this month's billing date − 1 is counted as this month's bill.
Example: Kiwi = 25 → Mar bill covers Feb 25 to Mar 24.
ℹ️ How it works
• Credit cards use transaction date to assign spend to the correct bill month
• UPI and Cash always use the calendar month (no billing cycle)
• The billing date pill 🗓25 shows on each card in the Dashboard
• Settings are saved in your browser and persist across sessions
